Influencer Promotion Brokerage Platform
Business Requirement & Policy Document (India)
1. Company Overview

Business Name: (Your Company Name)

Business Type: Influencer Marketing Brokerage Platform

Business Model:
The company acts as an intermediary between businesses and social media influencers. Businesses submit promotion requirements, and the company connects them with suitable influencers. The company earns a brokerage/service commission on every successful deal.

2. Business Workflow
Step 1 – Business Registration

Businesses register on the platform and provide:

Company Name
GST Number (if applicable)

Step 2 – Campaign Creation

Business submits:

Deliverables
Step 3 – Influencer Selection

The platform suggests influencers based on:

YouTube
Instagram
Facebook
X
LinkedIn
Snapchat
Other Platforms

Parameters:

Followers
Engagement Rate
Audience Location
Category
Previous Performance
Price
Step 4 – Deal Confirmation

Both parties accept:

Price
Deliverables
Posting Date
Content Requirements
Payment Terms

Digital agreement is generated.

Step 5 – Payment Collection

Business pays the total campaign amount to the company.

Example:

Campaign Value = ₹100,000

Company Commission (20%) = ₹20,000

Influencer Payment = ₹80,000

Company releases payment to the influencer only after deliverables are verified according to the agreement.

Step 6 – Campaign Verification

Verification includes:

Post Uploaded
Story Uploaded
Video Published
Link Added
Minimum Time Requirement
Screenshot Proof
Analytics Submission
Step 7 – Payment Release

After verification:

Company transfers the influencer's share.

Invoice is generated automatically.

Business Cancellation

Before influencer accepts:
100% refund (excluding processing fee, if any)

After acceptance:
Refund subject to work completed and agreement terms.

After campaign completion:
No refund unless contract terms provide otherwise.
